Democracy in the World

How does the health of democracy vary from country to country within a given continent? Using FreedomHouse data from 1973-2019, I generated country profiles for all instances where data was available. The profiles also incorporate economic indicators from the World Bank.

I am currently working to expand this analysis to include measurements of inequality, and to explore the relationship between income and democracy on a global scale.

These profiles are fully reproducible. Check it out on Github.
